Purpose of Lilac Mist

Lilac Mist is a local regional weather site. To obtain the latest updates on the weather conditions.
Knowing the local weather conditions can focus the community to plan local events.
Alert the local community to adverse weather conditions affecting events, or the needs
of the community in general, heat waves, storms, flooding, etc...
