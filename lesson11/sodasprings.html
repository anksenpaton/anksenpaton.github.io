Soda Springs Geyser

Soda Springs Geyser, is a captive geyser. This geyser was discovered when drilling for a swimming pool. This undertaking happened almost eighty years ago. It is now capped and controled with a timer to erupt every hour on the hour. Soda Springs Geyser reaches heights of up to one hundred feet, and is in action three hundred and sixty-five days a year. The public is welcome, there are picnic tables provided, as well as public restrooms. So bring a picnic lunch and plan a day out watching thia amazing natural wonder. Interperative signs also provided, and a boardwalk surround for closer viewing. Come to Soda Springs Idaho and enjoy all our natural wonders.
