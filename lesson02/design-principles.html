Visual Hierarchy
Google Maps
google.com/maps

The map is the largest visual on this page. My eyes go there first.
Next my eyes are drawn to the find a place input line.
Finally eyes see get directions at the bottom.

White Space and Clean Space
Amazon
amazon.com

The white empty spaces between the items are well defined.
The white spaces separate the items so My eyes can define one item from another.
The spaces are very clean not confusing, driving the focus to the individual products.

Rule of Thirds
The Vermont Country Store
www.vermontcountrystore.com

Using nice images, and text, and the search bar, the space is divided up into thirds sections.
Text in contrasting color on top, store name contrasting color, search bar.
Clean white space to focus next section. Two smaller images side by side, one in text information.
Finally one large image draws the eyes to the bottom of the page to give a hint of what is to come.
