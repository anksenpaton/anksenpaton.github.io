Fish Haven, an Undiscovered Paradise

FISH HAVEN, Idaho — It's an undiscovered paradise. Most people who drive by recognize the raw beauty of a place ringed by high desert mountains and bordered by the shores of a vast natural lake. In winter the hills outside this small paradise echo with the sharp whine of snow machines and in the summer thousands of tourists ply the lake's bright blue waters with water skis or fishing poles. (Author: Kurt Friedemann Idaho State Journal, Dec 26, 2000) (Picture: Matthew Trump / CC-BY-SA-3.0)
